Distance from Major Cities

Solapur

Distance: 45 km

Regular buses and taxis available.

Pune

Distance: 300 km

Direct buses and private taxis available.

Hyderabad

Distance: 300 km

Well connected by highway and bus routes.

Mumbai

Distance: 450 km

Travel by train to Solapur then taxi/bus.

Bus Transport

Maharashtra State Transport (MSRTC)

Bus Stand: Tuljapur Bus Stand

Regular MSRTC buses connect Tuljapur with Solapur, Pune, Latur, Osmanabad, and other cities.

Helpline: 1800-22-1250

Private Bus Services

Private buses operate from Pune, Mumbai, and Hyderabad to nearby cities.

From Solapur you can take a taxi or local bus to Tuljapur.

Taxi Services

Local Taxi Services

Pickup: Solapur Railway Station

Taxis are available for direct travel to the temple.
