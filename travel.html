By Road

Tuljapur is well connected by road to major cities like Solapur, Pune, Hyderabad, and Mumbai.

Distance from Major Cities

  • Solapur – 45 km
  • Pune – 300 km
  • Hyderabad – 300 km
  • Mumbai – 450 km
