Welcome to Tuljapur

About Tuljapur

Tuljapur is one of the most important pilgrimage destinations in Maharashtra. It is famous for the sacred Tulja Bhavani Temple dedicated to Goddess Bhavani. Devotees from across India visit Tuljapur to seek blessings from the goddess.

The temple is considered one of the 51 Shakti Peethas and holds great spiritual importance in Hindu tradition. Many devotees believe that Goddess Bhavani protects her followers and grants strength, courage, and prosperity.

Tulja Bhavani Temple

Tulja Bhavani Temple is located on the Balaghat mountain range in Tuljapur. The temple architecture reflects traditional Hindu temple design and has several gates, courtyards, and shrines.

The main deity of the temple is Goddess Bhavani, also known as Tulja Bhavani. The idol of the goddess is believed to be self-manifested and holds great religious significance.

It is believed that Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was a great devotee of Tulja Bhavani. According to legends, the goddess blessed him with a sword that helped him establish the Maratha Empire.
